About our phone

Qualcomm Snapdragon 855+ mobile platform
The octa-core processor and 8GB of RAM deliver outstanding overall performance for opening and running applications, flipping through menus, running home screens and more.

Android 10
Offers new ways to control your privacy, customize your phone, and get things done. It's Android, just the way you want it.

Optimized UX
Elegantly designed to fit your lifestyle (and your pocket), Galaxy Z Flip features an optimized UX, so you can put your best self forward.

Universal unlocked
Compatible with all major U.S. carriers, including Verizon, AT&T, Sprint, and T-Mobile. Also compatible with prepaid carriers, including Cricket Wireless, Boost Mobile, MetroPCS, Simple Mobile, Total Wireless, Tracfone, Net10, Mint, and H2O.

4G LTE speed
Provides fast web connection for downloading apps, streaming content, and staying connected to social media.

6.7" Dynamic AMOLED screen
Immersive and utterly seamless, the 6.7" ultrathin glass display is a quality foldable glass that flips closed to fit easily in a bag or pocket.

Transform the way you capture
Galaxy Z Flip's breakthrough camera lets you shoot, stream, and connect hands-free from new angles. With amped-up night shots, high-definition 4K video capture, and wide-angle perspectives, you can share your world whenever and wherever you are.

256GB internal memory
Provides storage space for your videos, music, photos and more.

Flex mode
Control select apps with one half of the screen while you preview them on the other with the Flex mode, elevating your experience as you chat, record, preview, and edit.

Cover display
Stay up to date even when Galaxy Z Flip is closed with convenient notifications that allow you to transition from the discreet external display to full screen with App Continuity.**

Multi-Active Window
Lets you navigate multiple apps on one display. Drag and drop between apps, or video chat and keep up with emails all at once with Galaxy Z Flip's adaptable, innovative split-screen experience.

Fingerprint ID
Galaxy Z Flip easily unlocks, whether folded or open, with a sensor located where your grip naturally lands.
